This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application Caesar Puzzle Calendar (“the App”) provided by Peter Ombodi (“we”, “us”, or “our”) handles user data. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

The App is designed as an offline puzzle game and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us or to third parties. In particular, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or other contact information
  • User accounts or passwords
  • Precise or approximate location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Payment information or financial data
  • Photos, media, or files outside the App’s own storage area
  • Analytics, usage statistics, or crash reports via third-party services

2. Data Stored Locally on the Device

To provide core game functionality, the App may store limited data locally on your device only, such as:

  • Current game progress and puzzle state
  • Basic game settings and preferences (for example, language or sound settings)

This data is processed solely on your device and is not transmitted to us or to any external servers.

3. No Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs or services such as:

  • Analytics or tracking services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social network integrations
  • Cloud storage or backend services

As a result, no personal or usage data is shared with third parties through the App.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for a general audience and does not contain content specifically directed at children. Since we do not collect personal data, we comply with applicable child data protection regulations, such as the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar laws in other jurisdictions.

5. Your Rights and Control

Because the App does not collect or store personal data on our servers, we do not have access to any information that could identify you. All game-related data is stored locally on your device and can be removed by uninstalling the App or clearing its data in your device settings.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example if the App’s functionality changes or if we integrate new services. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated “Last updated” date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
